Komposita
[kom-POH-zi-tah]
Compound Nouns — building new words by joining two or more words
💡 Note

The Golden Rule of Komposita:
The article of the compound always comes from the last noun.
The first letter of the second word stays lowercase when joined.
Colour coding: first part (blue) + last part (pink) = compound.
