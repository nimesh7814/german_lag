Talking About Your Hobbies

GermanEnglish
Was ist dein Hobby?What is your hobby?
Mein Hobby ist …My hobby is …
Was sind deine Hobbys?What are your hobbies?
Meine Hobbys sind …My hobbies are …
Was machst du gern?What do you like doing? (informal)
Was machen Sie gern?What do you like doing? (formal)
Was machst du nicht so gern?What do you not like doing?
Das mache ich sehr gern. 😊I like doing that very much.
Das mache ich nicht so gern. ☹️I don't really like doing that.

Gern / Nicht so gern — Examples

Ich schlafe sehr gern. — I really like sleeping.

Ich höre gerne Musik. — I like listening to music.

Ich sehe gerne Filme. — I like watching films.

Ich chatte nicht so gern. — I don't really like chatting.

Ich koche nicht so gern. — I don't really like cooking.

Ich gehe nicht so gern Camping. — I don't really like going camping.

Wortstellung — Word Order

🔴 Important

The Two Rules of German Word Order:
① The verb always stands at position 2.
② The subject stands at either position 1 or position 3.

Normal order (subject first):

Ich
Pos. 1 (Subj.)
gehe
Pos. 2 (Verb)
jeden Tag
Pos. 3
schwimmen.
rest

Inverted order (time adverb first):

Jeden Tag
Pos. 1 (Time)
gehe
Pos. 2 (Verb)
ich
Pos. 3 (Subj.)
schwimmen.
rest

Three ways to say the same sentence:

Du
gehst
dreimal pro Woche ins Fitness-Studio.
Dreimal pro Woche
gehst
du
ins Fitness-Studio.
Ins Fitness-Studio
gehst
du
dreimal pro Woche.

All three mean: You go to the gym three times a week.
